Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling is a Korean web novel and webtoon that follows Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter in a world where humans battle monsters in mysterious dungeons. One day, after a deadly dungeon raid, he gains a unique ability to "level up" like a game character, becoming stronger without limits. As he rises from the weakest to the most powerful, he uncovers hidden truths about the dungeons, the world, and his own destiny.
