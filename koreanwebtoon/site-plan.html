Images for the Home page

This Webtoon is Solo Leveling. The webtoon's genres are fantasy and action. The brief storyline is the following: The hero, who was the weakest boy on Earth, gains a skill that allows only him to level up his abilities. Using this skill, he gradually becomes stronger and fights monsters invading Earth. It is published on Webtoons.com and is available for free.

Searching page

I plan to make this page a detailed search page. It will help visitors search for webtoons with more specific options. The page will provide an overview of various webtoon types and include buttons that assist visitors in finding webtoons of their desired genres.

Images for the Page 2

detail page

I plan to make this page to contain detailed information about the webtoon selected by a visitor. When visitors select a webtoon they are interested in and click on its image, they will be directed to this page. Them, they can see more images of the webtoon, learn about its story, and find out which platforms offer it and at what price. Additionally, this page will introduce other webtoons in genres that visitors might like.

This Webtoon is Teenage Mercenary. The webtoon's genres are drama and action. The brief storyline is the following: The main charactor, who lived as a mercenary from a young age, escapes from his organization and tries to attend a normal school. As he goes to school, interesting events unfold. It is published on Webtoons.com and is available for free.
